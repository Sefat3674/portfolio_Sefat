Deep Learning-Based Analysis of Fundus Images for Early Detection of Retinal Diseases

IEEE Paper

Identification of Deceptive Selling Posts in Bangla Social Media Text

Introduced the first expert-annotated Bangla dataset of 1,790 Facebook selling posts labeled with a formal five-criterion deception rubric (F1–F5). Proposed RubricBanglaBERT — a feature-augmented architecture that injects structured binary deception indicators into BanglaBERT contextual representations. Annotation reliability confirmed via Fleiss' Kappa (κ = 0.81).
